## 3. CLIENT ACQUISITION CHANNELS

### A. LinkedIn Alpha Strategy (Primary)
*   **Action:** Daily "Insight Posts" rather than "Finished Work" posts.
*   **Content:** Breakdown of SaaS UX failures (e.g., "Why 90% of Dashboards Fail to Retain Users").
*   **Networking:** Identify 10 Founders/CTOs of Series A companies daily. Send personalized, low-friction Loom audits of their current product.

### B. Upwork / TopTal Positioning
*   **Profile Headline:** "Product Design Director for SaaS | Scalable Design Systems Expert."
*   **Proposal Logic:** Use the "Problem-First" approach. Never lead with your skills; lead with their friction points.
    *   *Sample:* "I noticed your booking flow has 4 steps; for Reserva Booking, we reduced this to 1, increasing conversion by 22%. Here's how we can apply that to your project..."

### C. Aesthetic Authority (Behance/Dribbble)
*   **Action:** Monthly "Mega-Case Study" drops.
*   **Focus:** Showcase the *Process* (The Alpha Protocol). Include UX audits, technical documentation, and motion prototypes, not just pretty screens.
